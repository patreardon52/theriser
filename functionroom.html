FUNCTION ROOM

Established in the late 1800s, the Rising Sun has done a great job of merging old fashioned service with modern facilities and styling.

The fully equipped Function Room boasts its own bar and a versatile layout where furniture can be arranged to suit your needs. The L-shaped space means that you can have a cohesive party, but enjoy two distinct zones for dancing versus mingling, or nibbling versus presentations.

The house-made party food will be a hit, as will the fab service and cosy decor.
